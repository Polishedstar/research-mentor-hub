MGT-15: Impact of Liquidity Mnagement on the Profitability of Organizations (A Study of Nasco Foods, Nigeria, Ltd)

Findings of the Research Work

From the analysis above, the researcher is presenting the following findings:

  1. Liquidity management has an impact on organizational profitability. For instance, idle funds do not yield any returns; therefore, idle funds should be invested.
  2. Shortage of liquidity equally affects the organization when maturing obligations are not met as when due. This will lead to a loss of customer or supplier confidence in the organization.
  3. Organizations should endeavor to strike a balance between keeping excessive liquidity (which will not yield any return) and keeping too little liquidity that cannot meet obligations (which will also have a negative impact on the organization).
