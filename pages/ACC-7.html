ACC-7: Relationship between Information and Communication Technology (ICT) and Banking Operations in Jos Metropolis

Findings of the Research Work

This study was designed particularly to find out the impact of ICT on banking operations with some selected UBA branches within Jos.

The study was carried out to determine the impact of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) on Banking Operations. Banking system has adopted the use of many ICT products (gadgets) for the efficient operations of its activities, this was shown by 96% of the responses who consent to this. And have adopted many strategies on the qualitative use of ICT in their daily transactions this was indicated by 95% of the responses who agreed to this. Epileptic Power Supply has also been regarded as the major constraints to effective ICT usage as it is indicated by 40% of the responses who accept it to be a major constraint.

The study was spread over five chapters. The research was introduced in Chapter One which focused on problem statement, objectives, statement of hypothesis, significance, and limitations. In Chapter two, the theoretical basis was laid with the review of the concepts, theories, and empirical studies. Chapter three focused on the methodology for the actual conduct of the study, with chapter four presenting the data, its analysis and interpretation which included the test of hypothesis and concluded with discussion of findings. Chapter five concerned with the summary, conclusions, recommendations, and suggestions for further study.
