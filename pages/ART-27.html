ART-27: Effects of Social Media on Consumer Buying Behaviour (A Case Study of Jumia Nigeria Ltd)

Findings of the Research Work

  • FaceBook has a significant effect on consumer buying behavior towards Jumia Nigeria, Jos.
  • Twitter has a significant effect on consumer buying behavior towards Jumia Nigeria, Jos.
  • YouTube has a significant effect on consumer buying behavior towards Jumia Nigeria, Jos.

This study examined the effect of social media on consumer buying behavior of Jumia Nigeria in Jos Metropolis by analyzing primary data collected from sampled customers of Jumia Nigeria in Jos. The results obtained from the study indicate that FaceBook, Twitter, and YouTube have significant effects on consumer buying behavior towards Jumia Nigeria in Jos. Hence, the study concludes that there exists a significant relationship between social media and consumer buying behavior in today's contemporary marketing environment.
