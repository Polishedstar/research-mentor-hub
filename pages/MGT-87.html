MGT-87: Impact of Inventory Control System on Organizational Efficiency in Manufacturing Organizations

Findings of the Research Work

This research work seeks to review the impact of the inventory control system in business organizations. Based on the findings and analysis of this study, the use of a computerized tracking system has contributed to the increase in productivity and maintaining competitiveness. This research has examined the impact of inventory control systems on business organizations, highlighting efficient utilization of working capital, improvement in customer relationships, and the resulting economic growth and development.

Based on the findings of this research work, one can conclude that the inventory control system has helped business organizations properly monitor their inventory to avoid stockouts or surplus. This, in turn, improves customer relationships and fulfillment rates due to smooth and uninterrupted production of goods and services, facilitated by well-defined internal control and competent officers responsible for managing inventory.
