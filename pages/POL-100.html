POL-100: Corruption and its Impact on Rural Development

Findings of the Research Work

Major findings of the study are summarized as follows:

  1. It was discovered that corruption in Barkin Ladi local government is very high.
  2. The findings revealed that Barkin Ladi local government has provided development to the citizens.
  3. It was discovered that the study provides an understanding of the pro-activeness and effectiveness of local government in addressing the critical problems of corruption in the public service, including the local government councils in Nigeria.
  4. The findings revealed that to a large extent, corruption has affected the development of the area.
  5. It was discovered that the findings of the study expose the negative effect of rural development and the extent to which corrupt practices have affected the performance of most local government councils in Nigeria.
