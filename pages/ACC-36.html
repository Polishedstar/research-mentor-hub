ACC-36: Entrepreneurship and Poverty Eradication in Nigeria (A Case Study of Jos South Local Government Area)

Findings of the Research Work

  1. Entrepreneurship education has an impact on community development in Jos South Local Government.
  2. Entrepreneurship is seen as a process which involves the effort of an individual or individuals in identifying viable business opportunities.
  3. The aim of entrepreneurship development is to enlarge the base of entrepreneurs in an economy.
  4. Poverty may arise from low productivity of the households.
  5. Poverty is more than a lack of income.
  6. Entrepreneurship development contributes to poverty reduction.
  7. Entrepreneurship development creates employment through the establishment of new businesses.
  8. Acquisition of vocational skills leads to a significant reduction of poverty among young adults.
  9. Entrepreneurship is viewed today as a key driver of economic growth.
  10. The federal government has initiated several measures and policies to reduce the level of poverty among the masses.
