ACC-3: The Role of Commercial Banks in Industrial Development in Jos (A Case Study of Union Bank of Nigeria, Plc)

Findings of the Research Work

This evaluative study spans five chapters to highlight the role of commercial banks in industrial development in Jos. The research is limited to Union Bank Plc, Jos Branch but is intended for those directly or indirectly involved in the banking sector.

Chapter one introduces the subject matter of this research. In the process of this study, it presents the statement of the problem where the identified effects were stated. Additionally, it outlines the purpose of this study. The chapter discusses the significance of the study, the scope and limitations, research hypotheses, methods of carrying out the research, and the area (scope) to be covered in the research work.

Chapter two focuses on the works of other scholars. It presents different views, concepts, and opinions expressed by various writers and authorities on the bank's mediating role of fund mobilization. The chapter also reviews different people's work on the topic.

Chapter three contains details of how the research work was carried out. It includes information on the population of the study, determination of the sample size, methods of data collection, and how the surveyed data were sourced using primary and secondary data collection methods. It also describes the data analysis process.

Lastly, chapter four contains the methods used to analyze the sourced data, specifically the chi-square (X2) method.
