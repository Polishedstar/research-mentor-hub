HLT-15: The Role of Health Information Management in achieving Service Delivery Care in Nigeria (A Study of Plateau State Specialist Hospital, Jos)

Findings of the Research Work

This study was structured into five chapters. Chapter one looked into the background of the study identified. The problems of the study, objective of the study were clearly stated, research questions and hypothesis to guide the investigation were formulated. The chapter also highlighted the justification and significance of the study, the scope of the study were also stated. The study variables also defined terms relevant to the study were defined based on their contextual use in the work.

The chapter two dealt with three basic components of the study. These are the conceptual framework which emphasized the historical background of Health Information Management, Communication, Database as Integral to healthcare, Software in Service Delivery care ICT, Medical Hardware, Purpose of Communication and other aspects relevant to Health Information Management, framework and summary of the review.
