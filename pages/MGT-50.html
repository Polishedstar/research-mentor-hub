MGT-50: Small Scale Business and its Impact on the Economic Development of Plateau State

Findings of the Research Work

  1. Shahara Engineering Company still encourages the acquisition of skills for the intending small scale business in Nigeria.
  2. Shahara Engineering Company invested in creating a positive impact on small scale businesses.
  3. Small Scale Enterprises constitute the very basis of the national economy.
  4. Small Scale Enterprises contribute to the development of local technology.
  5. Small Scale Enterprises provide an effective means of stimulating indigenous entrepreneurship.
  6. Small Scale Enterprises contribute to the development of the state (Plateau) in terms of infrastructure, employment creation, and the like.
  7. Small Scale Enterprises ensure the structural balance in terms of large and small industry sectors.
  8. Small Scale Enterprises ensure the supply of high-quality parts and components and intermediate products, thereby strengthening the international competitiveness of manufacturers' goods.
  9. Small Scale Enterprises stimulate technological development and innovations, produce specialized items in small quantities to meet current and diverse demands.

From the responses obtained from the questionnaire analyzed, the researcher has come to the conclusion that Shahara Engineering Company is creating a positive impact and has played an important role in the development of small-scale business.

It will now be reasonable to emphasize that Shahara Engineering Company is really contributing to the development of small-scale business, which encourages the nation's economy.
