MGT-152: The Effect of Sales Promotion on Organizational Sales Volume

Findings of the Research Work

The research work has gone a long way to define problems that are attached to the study and possible solutions to the problems. The topic of this case study was chosen to find out how sales promotion has improved the goods and services of Nigerian Bottling Company Plc.

The major objectives of sales promotion activities in a business organization are to generate and increase sales of a product or services through advertising, personal selling, and sales promotion.

In view of the research findings on the study, this project or research work has been successful in highlighting the comparative effectiveness of sales promotion. It has also verified the users' understanding of the effectiveness of this as one of the promotional tools in the marketing of products.

In addition, sales promotion programs, as the name implies, are designed to increase sales and promote the sales of products or services. Effective and good sales promotion requires imagination and creativity, coupled with a sound foundation in marketing fundamentals. Sales promotion activities also include the development and management of premiums, giveaways, contests, product sampling, and other types of promotion without being told.

Nigerian Bottling Company Plc has an excellent reputation and is known for its services and high quality. The investigation and findings have evaluated people's perception of sales promotion.

The management of NBC Plc has come up with a very efficient, effective, and interesting sales promotion campaign, which will help their company meet their targets. This is because good sales promotion programs directly lead to positive turnover, whether in productivity or profitability.
