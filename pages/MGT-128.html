MGT-128: Changing Company's Future through Organized Marketing Strategies

Findings of the Research Work

The role of marketing strategy in changing a company's fortune cannot be over-emphasized. The researcher based on analysis found out that there is an increased relationship in the success of marketing an organization's production or product with its marketing planning and control. Also, based on the second hypothesis between training, marketing personnel for effective and efficient marketing management.

It was gathered from the responses of the respondents who filled the questionnaire that the organization can survive without marketing activities. Both primary and secondary data are used for this research work. Also, gathered is information about the company's advertisement; that the company mostly uses radio as their advertising channel for creating awareness. Moreover, the finding reveals that the advertising position of the company's product is strong in the mind of the consumer in order to encourage repeated purchase of the product.

This project work evaluates the imperative of marketing strategies towards the achievement of organizational goals and to create more prospects for the organization. This is with particular reference to Nigerian Associated Best Foods Limited, Jos.

This study has revealed how the marketing department of Nigerian Associated Best Foods Limited carries out their marketing activities and how it is coordinated to achieve overall goals and objectives.

This project work has given a rare opportunity to have an insight into Nigerian Associated Best Foods Limited, Jos, to assess and understand how their marketing in an organization is disposed off to prospective buyers and to ensure customer satisfaction.
