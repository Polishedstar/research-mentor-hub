EDU-16: Impact of SUBEB on the Management of Public Primary Schools in Mangu LGA

Findings of the Research Work

Based on the analysis in this research, the following are the summary of findings:

  1. That the availability of teaching and learning materials enhances the achievement of quality basic education in public primary schools in Mangu Local Government Area.
  2. That steps taken in ensuring teacher quality and staff development in the achievement of quality basic education in public primary schools in Mangu Local Government Area include government utilization of the potentials of ministry of education officials to train and retrain teachers to increase the improvement of quality basic education.
  3. That the availability of funding enhances quality basic education in public primary schools in Mangu Local Government Area.
  4. That good government policies and programs in enhancing quality basic education in public primary schools in Mangu Local Government Area affect quality basic education to a large extent.
  5. That the availability of infrastructures affects the provision of quality basic education in public primary schools in Mangu Local Government Area.
