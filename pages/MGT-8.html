MGT-8: The Impact of Radio Advertisement on the Sales Volume of Consumer Goods (A Case study of PRTVC Ltd, Jos)

Findings of the Research Work

Based on the analysis in this research, below are the summaries of findings:

  1. That radio advertisement has an impact on the sales volume of consumer goods.
  2. That radio advertisement helped in the sales of consumer goods.
  3. That advertisement inflates the price of a commodity.
  4. That there is a positive effect of radio advertisement on the behavior of the general public.
  5. That consumer goods producers experience high income due to advertisement.
  6. That the functional level of advertisement is high for the sales volume of consumer goods.
  7. That advertisement has an effect on the production of goods and services.
  8. That advertisement is a persuasive tool with the dangerous potential to create consumer needs.
  9. That radio advertisement increases the profitability of an organization.

The messages of organizations, most especially, aim to persuade their customers to patronize their products. Hence, there is a tendency for the success of the advertised product to increase the volume of sales.
