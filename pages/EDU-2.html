EDU-2: Impact of Resource Management on Students' Academic Performance in Secondary Schools in Jos North and Bassa Education Zones

Findings of the Research Work

This study assessed the Impact of resources management on students' performance in public secondary schools in Jos North and Bassa education zones in Plateau State. The study was carried out with six objectives which include to:

  1. Assess the impact of human resources management on students' performances in public secondary schools in Jos North and Bassa education zones in Plateau State.
  2. Ascertain the impact of material resources management on students' performance in public secondary schools in Jos North and Bassa education zones in Plateau State.
  3. Find out the impact of curriculum contents on students' performance in public secondary schools in Jos North and Bassa education zones in Plateau State.

The study adopted the descriptive survey design. The population of the study is made up of one thousand eight hundred and fifty-seven (1857) respondents, comprising eighty-one (81) principals, one thousand seven hundred and fifty-five (1755) teachers, and twenty-one (21) supervisors from both Jos North and Bassa education zones, respectively. A sample size of twenty-five (25) principals, five hundred and twenty-seven (527) teachers, and seven (7) supervisors were sampled for the study, making a total of five hundred and fifty-nine (559) respondents. This sample size was arrived at using stratified sampling technique.

The instrument used for data collection in this study is a questionnaire. The reliability of the instrument was determined using Cronbach Alpha technique, which showed a reliability index of 0.84. The bio-data of the respondents was analyzed using descriptive statistics in the form of tables, frequency distribution, and percentages to present and describe the pattern of the demographic characteristics of the respondents. In order to answer the research questions, mean and standard deviation were used, while the six null hypotheses were analyzed using Analysis of Variance (ANOVA) at the 0.05 alpha level of significance.

The respondents differ in their opinions on the impact of human resources management on students' performance in public secondary schools in Jos North and Bassa education zones in Plateau State.

  1. The respondents unanimously agreed that material resources management impacted on students' performance in public secondary schools in Jos North and Bassa education zones in Plateau State.
  2. The respondents agreed that curriculum contents impacted on students' performance in public secondary schools in Jos North and Bassa education zones in Plateau State.
  3. The respondents agreed financial resources have a significant impact on students' performance in public secondary schools in Jos North and Bassa education zones in Plateau State.
  4. The respondents agreed that time management impacted on students' performance in public secondary schools in Jos North and Bassa education zones in Plateau State.
  5. The respondents agreed that instructional materials management impacted on teaching and learning in public secondary schools in Jos North and Bassa education zones in Plateau State.
