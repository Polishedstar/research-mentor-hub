ART-28: E-Learning and E-Readiness Adoption as Correlates of Rapid Demand for Publis University Education in Nigeria

Findings of the Research Work

The study showed that e-learning had no significant effect on the rapid demand for public university education in Nigeria. The study also revealed that e-readiness had a significant effect on the rapid demand for public university education in Nigeria. The study further indicated that e-learning and e-readiness had a significant effect on the rapid demand for public university education in Nigeria.

The findings of this study led to the conclusion that e-learning does not lead to an increase in the rapid demand for public university education in Nigeria. The study also concluded that e-readiness does lead to an increase in the rapid demand for public university education in Nigeria. The study further concluded that e-learning and e-readiness do lead to an increase in the rapid demand for public university education in Nigeria.
