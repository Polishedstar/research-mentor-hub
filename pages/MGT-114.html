MGT-114: Marketing Strategies in a Competitive Environment (A Case Study of Nasco Food Nigeria Ltd)

Findings of the Research Work

This project, "Distribution as a Tool for Effective Marketing in a Production Industry," focuses on uncovering the importance and viability of distribution in enhancing the supreme efficiency of consumer goods, particularly in firms within the production industry. The primary focus is on Nasco Food Products. The research work is structured in chapters, each with a specific purpose.

Chapter One outlines the background of the study, providing context for why distribution of goods and services is necessary. It also addresses the statement of the problem, objectives of the study, research questions, hypothesis statements, and the significance of the study. The scope and limitations of the study are also discussed.

Chapter Two, the literature review, delves into what distribution entails, as explained by various scholars. It covers topics such as market distribution planning, its role in marketing, and its importance to a firm's overall strategy. Distribution channel objectives are linked to the application of the marketing mix as part of an integrated marketing plan. The choice of distribution channel by Nasco Food Products is also explored.

Chapter Three focuses on the methodology, detailing the methods of data collection and their procedures. The population under consideration is discussed, along with the sample size used. The validity and reliability of the instruments used are tested and confirmed by professionals in the field. The study area is Jos South Local Government.

Chapter Four delves into the presentation and interpretation of data, as well as the testing of hypotheses.

Finally, Chapter Five summarizes the findings and draws conclusions regarding the importance of distribution in the marketing of Nasco Food Products Ltd. Recommendations are made for enhanced performance by the management when embarking on distribution efforts. The limitations of the study are also discussed.
