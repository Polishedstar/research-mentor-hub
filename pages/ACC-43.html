ACC-43: The Effect of Global Financial Crises on the Performance of Financial Institutions (A Case Study of First Bank, Nigeria Plc)

Findings of the Research Work

After completing all the processes of carrying out the research, we present here our findings:

  1. Global economic meltdown has a negative impact on the performance of manufacturing industries, particularly Coca-Cola.
  2. Coca-Cola plc witnessed a high increase in unsold stocks and an increase in bank rates and charges as a result of the global economic meltdown.
  3. The turnover of Coca-Cola and profit margin of 2005-2006 was higher than that of 2007-2010, which witnessed the effects of the global economic recession.
  4. Just like most countries, the Nigerian economy suffered from the global economic meltdown, particularly the financial market and manufacturing.
  5. Despite the global economic meltdown, businesses can still record huge success in their transactions through diversification of the investment portfolio.
  6. Potential manufacturers were discouraged from engaging in small-scale businesses during the economic meltdown period due to an increase in interest rates and bank charges.
