MGT-27: Impact of Small Scale Enterprises on Growth and Development of a Nation's Economy

Findings of the Research Work

From the responses obtained from the questions under analysis, the researcher has come to the conclusion that small-scale enterprises create a positive impact on the growth and development of a nation's economy.

In the course of this research, the researcher realizes some positive effects of small-scale enterprises in the creation of a positive impact on the economy:

  1. (a) Small-scale enterprises contribute to the economic growth and development of Nigeria.
  2. (b) Small-scale enterprises have a strong impact on the nation's Gross Domestic Product and contribute to a variety of products for consumers in the marketplace.
  3. (c) Small-scale businesses are significantly contributing to employment creation and are also used as a tool to alleviate poverty in Nigeria.

In the past chapters, the researcher has tried to examine the impact of small-scale enterprises on the growth and development of a nation's economy. The researcher also looked at how they have been contributing and also the problems associated with small-scale business in Nigeria.
