MGT-10: The Impact of Entrepreneurship Training on Entrepreneurial Development in Plateau State

Findings of the Research Work

From the research conducted, the findings can be summarized as follows:

  • Through the training provided by the NDE, youth were able to become self-employed. This had a positive impact by:
    1. Increasing their income and diversifying their income base.
    2. Strengthening their individual capacity to meet their needs and fostering a greater interest in their work to achieve self-reliance.
    3. Maintaining a sense of belonging as they operate their own businesses, enabling them to make independent decisions and be answerable to nobody.
  • The study also revealed that youth play a significant role in our society, both within and outside of it. Most of the respondents emphasized that without the youth, our country could face total collapse since they make up a significant portion of the labor force. In response to this challenge, the federal government initiated programs to ensure that a substantial number of youths are trained to adapt to the demands of modern society.
