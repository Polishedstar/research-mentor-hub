MGT-26: Communication System and its Impact on Organizational Performance

Findings of the Research Work

Based on the results from Chapter Four of the study, the following views about the impact of communication on organizational performance can be derived:

  1. The performance of workers is dependent on effective communication. A free and efficient flow of information and a well-established communication system lead to increased worker performance and accuracy compared to situations with communication barriers.
  2. The environment of an organization affects communication. Organizational structure plays a significant role in how communication is conducted. Well-structured organizations with hierarchies may have more formal communication processes, whereas less structured organizations may have faster but less formal communication.
  3. Communication influences the peace and stability within an organization. Good and effective communication allows individuals to share information, views, feelings, and perceptions, promoting peace and enhancing stability within the organization.
  4. Both private and public organizations experience lapses or barriers in the flow of information. Communication management issues can arise in various organizations, leading to communication challenges.
  5. Communication is a vital instrument for promoting the effective and efficient performance of workers within an organization.
