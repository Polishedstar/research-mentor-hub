POL-41: Evaluation of Manpower Training and Employees Productivity in Industrial Training Fund Headquaters, Jos

Findings of the Research Work

The summary of findings made from this research study includes that the Plateau State Ministry of Education benefits from manpower planning. Consequently, the organization has a department or unit responsible for manpower planning. It can also be inferred from the findings that manpower planning is a tool for effective organizational growth and productivity.

In addition, the findings reveal that manpower planning is an activity geared towards the utilization of employees in the organization. Furthermore, it confirms that manpower planning is vital in the management of the organizational workforce. The findings have also shown that sound manpower planning can lead to high productivity in the organization. Moreover, the findings revealed that manpower planning has a significant effect on organizational growth.

In conclusion, it is observed that manpower planning should be maintained in the organization as it is a tool for organizational advancement. Therefore, manpower planning is necessary for a service-oriented organization, such as the Plateau State Ministry of Education, Jos.

Manpower planning is indispensable in a sound organization considering its significance for the growth of such organizations. Therefore, the need to sustain efficiency in the organization cannot be overemphasized.

The management of the organization should always work towards the realization of the goals of the organization with proper manpower planning. In the analysis, the research proves that manpower planning is a machinery for organizational advancement; therefore, it should be maintained in the organization, such as the Plateau State Ministry of Education, Jos.
