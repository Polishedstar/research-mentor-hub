ACC-27: An Evaluation of Electronic Banking in Nigeria (A Study of Unity Bank, Jos)

Findings of the Research Work

The study was carried out in order to evaluate the impact of Electronic banking system in Unity Bank PLC. The general introductory aspect shed more light on the essentials of Electronic banking. Many literature and academic publications from different authors in Electronic banking, product emerging issues in electronic devices were looked at. In the course of this research, the researcher was able to find out that the origin of Electronic banking system is a conventional banking system which started in Nigeria in 1952.

Also, the prospects of Electronic banking were analyzed, in which Electronic banking improved the patronage and relationship of customers in Unity Bank PLC. This was achieved by adopting the Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN) banking guidelines.
