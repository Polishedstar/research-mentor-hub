ACC-44: The Influence of Environmental Factors on the Profitability of Commercial Banks

Findings of the Research Work

After all the processes of carrying out this research, the researcher presents here the findings:

  1. External environmental factors such as customers, competitors, suppliers, technology, government laws and regulations, legal conditions, political factors, social and cultural norms and beliefs are key to the continuous existence and growth of Nasco Group of Companies.
  2. There is a significant relationship between businesses and environmental factors in Nigeria. In order to survive and grow, the organization must operate at a profit. In order to operate at a profit, they must react appropriately to the external environmental factors within the operating economy.
  3. The rate at which external environmental factors influence business operations cannot be overemphasized. These factors provide both threats and opportunities. In most cases, they provide threats to the survival and growth of firms.
  4. It is worthy of note to say factors like competition, customer behavior, technological know-how, government policies, laws and regulations are the most challenges faced by Nasco Group of Companies amongst many other external factors.
