MGT-141: The Effect of Distribution Strategy on the Sales Volume of Blocks

Findings of the Research Work

Based on the findings, it was revealed that the distribution strategy of block industries has a significant effect on the sales volume of blocks. The key findings are:

  1. Distribution strategy is effective in their block industry. However, there are limiting factors affecting the operation of distribution strategies in their block industry.
  2. The block industry encounters lapses during the distribution of blocks, and the general economic situation in the country affects the distribution strategy in block industries.
  3. The distribution strategy used by block industries helps in achieving the objectives of the block industry, and the distribution strategy may have a specific time of operation.

From what has been discussed so far, it is evident that distribution strategy has a significant effect on the sale of products. Therefore, the researcher concludes that the success of Endevpro today is a result of a proper distribution strategy implemented by the company. As such, any manufacturer that wants to succeed in selling its products must excel in its distribution strategy.
