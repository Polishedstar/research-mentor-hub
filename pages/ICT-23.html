ICT-23: Effects of Ethics and Social Responsibilities on the Job Performance of Office Managers in Business Organizations (A Case Study of Plateau State Secretariat, Jos)

Findings of the Research Work

This study was carried out to examine the effects of ethics and social responsibilities on the job performance of office managers in a business organization. The objectives of the study were to:

  1. Determine the relationship between workplace ethics and office managers' performance in business organizations.
  2. Examine the consequences of not implementing the right workplace ethics on office managers' job performance in a business organization.
  3. Assess the importance of social responsibilities on the job performance of office managers in a business organization.

The entire study population was adopted for the study, since the population was not too large. The questionnaire was the instrument used for data collection, the instrument was subjected to face and content validation by the researcher's supervisor. Data collected for the study were analyzed using the mean rating on a five-point Likert scale.

The study revealed the following findings among others:

Relationship between Workplace Ethics and Office Managers' Performance:

  • Ethics and social responsibility enhance the job performance of office managers in business organizations.
  • Office managers with good ethics are more efficient in business organizations.
  • An organization must practice good ethics to achieve higher performance.
  • Workplace ethics should be a fundamental management concern at all levels of a business organization.
  • Workplace ethics is about what is morally correct, honorable, and acceptable to a large majority of people in a business organization.

Consequences of Not Implementing the Right Workplace Ethics:

  • Non-implementation of the right workplace ethics is the reason for the legal trouble of the entire organization.
  • Unethical behavior can lead to increased sales and profits.
  • Office managers who disobey the ethics of their organizations are not considered responsible and dependable assets to their organizations.
  • Non-implementation of the right workplace ethics can destroy the organization's hard-earned reputation.
  • Implementation of the right workplace ethics leads to better performance by office managers.

Importance of Social Responsibility on Office Managers' Job Performance:

  • Business organizations that are socially responsible perform better.
  • A business organization has a social responsibility of conducting its business in a manner that benefits society.
  • Socially responsible companies should adopt policies that promote the well-being of society and the environment while lessening negative impacts on them.
  • Job performance is a vital element of any organization.
  • Job performance is the most important factor for the success of an organization.
