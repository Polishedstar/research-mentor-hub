In line with these findings, Asur and Huberman (2010) indicated that SNS have affected the dynamics of our social groups and friendships.

In Table Three, it was discovered that social media affects the student learning behaviors to a very high extent. These findings tally with Kaitlin (2010) who said that social networking sites also affect the way we receive information and news as well as our learning behaviors.
