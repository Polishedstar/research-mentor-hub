MGT-100: Entrepreneurship Development and its Impact on Employment Generation

Findings of the Research Work

After all the processes of carrying out the research have been concluded, our findings are presented here:

  • The research found that entrepreneurship development has created job opportunities for people.
  • Entrepreneurship plays a vital role in the Nigerian economy.
  • The employees of the organization are engaged in training and development.
  • Loans are given to people who are trained in entrepreneurship to start up their businesses.
  • Entrepreneurship development has created great employment opportunities and had a positive impact on people's lives.
  • People who are trained in entrepreneurship become self-reliant.
  • There is a relationship between entrepreneurship and employment generation.

Conclusively, based on the research findings, entrepreneurship, despite some lingering problems, has had a positive impact on people's lives and the economy as a whole.

The research has also shown that entrepreneurship development has reduced social vices among the youth to the barest minimum by creating employment opportunities.

The various skills acquisition programs introduced by the federal government have played a vital role in creating job opportunities for people.
