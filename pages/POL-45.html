POL-45: Insecurity and Rural Development in Jos Sourth LGA

Findings of the Research Work

From the data presentation and analysis in the previous chapter, the following were discovered:

  1. Insecurity has a negative effect on rural development in Jos South Local Government Area of Plateau state.
  2. The current security measures put in place for the protection of life and property in Jos South Local Government Area of Plateau state are not effective.
  3. The incessant Fulani-herdsmen attacks in Jos South LGA have impacted negatively on the safety and well-being of the citizens.
  4. Fulani-herdsmen attacks have forced farmers to abandon farms and markets, leading to rising inflation due to disrupted agriculture.
  5. The inability of the government to initiate various programs to tackle security challenges across the state has contributed to increasing insecurity in the local government.
  6. Government and security agency efforts to curtail insurgency have not been adequate in the nation.
  7. The increasing rate of poverty, unemployment, political corruption, and poor government response to insecurity situations are the major causes of insurgency in Jos South Local Government.
  8. Most people have been rendered homeless, and farmlands and crops destroyed due to incessant attacks by the insurgency in Jos South LGA.
  9. Government must be proactive in dealing with security issues and threats.
