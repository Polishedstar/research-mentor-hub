MGT-92: Product Branding and Customers Patronage (A Case Study of Cadbury Nigeria Plc)

Findings of the Research Work

This study is an attempt to investigate the influence of product branding on consumer patronage with particular reference to Cadbury Nigeria Plc, Jos. It is also aimed at identifying certain basic problems that may be associated with brand management and recommend solutions to those problems.
