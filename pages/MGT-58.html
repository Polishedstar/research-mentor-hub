MGT-58: The Effect of Radio Advertisement on the Sales Volume of Consumer Goods (A Case Study of PRTVC Ltd, Jos)

Findings of the Research Work

Five chapters made up this study. Wherein Chapter one of the research work, problems of the study were stated as well as the background of the study. In addition to this chapter, objectives of the study were stated; significance of the study was stated, and the scope of the study was introduced in chapter one.

In Chapter two, literature review was carried out, different theories that formed the background upon which Radio advertisement was built, as well as the effects of Radio advertisement, were all examined. Profitable advertisement was found.

Chapter three presented the research methodology, sampling techniques, data collection instrument, and techniques employed for data analysis were all discussed. An organized form of research methodology was used. Questionnaires were employed, and the population was studied.

In the course of this study, it was discovered that Radio advertisement is an important means of creating awareness and promoting the sales of a product.

This study investigated ways of enhancing sales of a product through advertisement. Advertisers use catchy headlines, illustrations, or graphics that could help convey the message of an organization, especially to persuade people to patronize their products.

In Chapter one, some specific objectives were set, and in the process of the research work, each of those stated objectives was realized. In the literature review, the problems militating against Radio advertisement were identified alongside enhancing sales of a product.

Mainly included in Chapter four were the research findings that revealed the significance of Radio Advertisement. Hence, there is a tendency for the success of the advert produced to increase the volume of the sales.
