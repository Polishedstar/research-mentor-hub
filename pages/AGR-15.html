AGR-15: Impact of Distribution Stategies on the Marketing of Irish Potato in Plateau State

Findings of the Research Work

This study accepts the H1 and rejects the H0, which means the distribution strategy in the marketing of Irish potato in Plateau is greatly influenced by the middlemen (intermediaries), and it is also very expensive according to Table 9. Furthermore, a lot of the respondents are of the opinion that some of the middlemen need to be eliminated from the channels of distribution. For example, Table 10 and Table 11 show that 35% are agents and merchants, 27% are wholesalers, 23% are cooperative societies, and 15% are retailers.

However, hawkers of the product are very important in the distribution channels. No respondent wants them out of the channel of distribution.
