MGT-30: The Impact of Television Advertisement on Consumer Buying Behavior in New Products

Findings of the Research Work

After all processes of carrying out the research have been concluded, we present here our findings on how television advertisement should be carried out in an organization:

  • It motivates consumers' purchasing decisions on the product through television advertisement.
  • For any organization to succeed in this global and competitive environment, it is essential to effectively and efficiently reach the target audience or consumers through appropriate mediums.
  • There is a need for organizations to engage the services of knowledgeable and well-trained professionals who can effectively carry out these functions on their behalf.
  • Companies should ensure consistency in television advertisements to promote their products and retain customers in the face of competition.
  • The organization should also strive to understand the buying patterns of their customers to achieve their goals and objectives successfully.
