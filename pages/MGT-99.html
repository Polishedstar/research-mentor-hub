MGT-99: Impact of Communication System on Organizational Performance (A Case Study of Guarantee Trust Bank, Nig, Ltd)

Findings of the Research Work

Based on the result from chapter four of the study, we could come out with the following views about the impact of communication on organizational performance:

  1. The performance of workers is dependent on effective communication. With a free and efficient flow of information and a well-established communication system, workers perform more accurately than when there is a communication barrier.
  2. The organization's environment affects communication. A well-structured organization with hierarchical communication channels may appear tedious but maintains a high degree of professionalism. In contrast, less organized organizations may have faster information flow but lack formality.
  3. Communication affects the peace in an organization. Effective communication allows individuals to share information, views, and feelings, promoting peace and stability.
  4. There are lapses in communication management in both private and public organizations. Barriers to information flow can be found in some organizations.
  5. Communication is a vital instrument in promoting effective and efficient worker performance in an organization.

This research work has explored the communication system and its impact on organizational performance, emphasizing the importance of communication in the job/work performance of workers in an organization. Although the study is not comprehensive in itself, it serves as a foundation for further research in related areas of study.
