MGT-1: Managerial Skills and Organizational Productivity

Findings of the Research Work

Based on the analysis of the results, the following findings were obtained:

a. That managerial skill is very essential in any organizational set up because human beings are far from perfect, therefore human efforts and material resources need to be controlled and geared towards the attainment of an organization's set goals.

b. That there are three managerial skills that must be present in an organization in order to achieve its set goals, which are human, conceptual, and technical skills.

c. That managerial skill can lead to greater job performance by an organization.

d. That managerial skill can lead to job performance through qualitative and high production by clear-defined policies, drives procedures, and clear audits, budgets, and special reports compiled by those concerned in the course of doing their jobs.

MGT-80: The Impact of Packaging on Consumers Purchasing Decisions

Findings of the Research Work

The contributions which product packaging has made to marketing industries and its impacts on consumers' purchasing decisions have developed the country's economy. Product packaging by industries in Nigeria is a good ground for development in the society as a choice.

The research discovered that product packaging is a vital tool used to boost sales.

It was confirmed that there is high demand for goods and services by consumers despite the high price of goods for any company that has a good strategic packaging style.

It also observed that companies tend to have low returns where there is poor packaging because there will be low patronage.

Product packaging plays a very vital role in consumers' purchase decisions because it attracts the customer's attention through good color design.

In the course of this research work, the summaries of findings are based on the data presentation and analysis of data which are presented in the form of frequency tables from the previous chapter. These include:

  1. It reveals that the role of packaging in consumer decision-making process cannot be overemphasized as consumers judge the product based on its packaging.
  2. It was confirmed that packaging plays a vital role in stimulating, retaining, as well as winning the interest of new products and consumers to purchase a company's products. Companies that have been using this have witnessed tremendous increases in sales.
  3. The findings show that it is imperative for companies to design and package their products well, including the company's name, logo, trademark, and so on.

It also indicates that failure to pay attention to packaging by companies will lead to poor and unpleasant consequences, such as the inability to achieve company goals, loss of goodwill, excess stock levels, low sales, and hence low profitability.
