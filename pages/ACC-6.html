ACC-6: Auditing and Investigation and its Impact as it affects Plateau State Board of Internal Revenue

Findings of the Research Work

Having examined all the processes of carrying out the research work, the following summary of findings are presented:

  1. Plateau State Board of Internal Revenue (PSBIR), Jos, frequently presents all necessary information to aid Auditors in expressing their opinions.
  2. Auditors are very sincere in reporting their findings to the management of PSBIR, Jos.
  3. PSBIR, Jos frequently carries out internal checks to detect and prevent any form of irregularities.
  4. Auditing and investigation of records and accounts of PSBIR, Jos have assisted the organization in achieving most of its aims and objectives.
  5. Management and auditors of PSBIR, Jos are relating very well in all aspects of activities.

Auditors are doing a great job in the examination of records and accounts of PSBIR, Jos.
