POL-2: Staff Motivation and Organizational Efficiency in Jos South LGC of Plateau State

Findings of the Research Work

Based on the discoveries made in the study, the following findings were drawn:

  1. Staff motivation has an impact on organization efficiency.
  2. Inadequate provision of infrastructure, poor welfare services, lack of supervision, poor communication, corruption, and mismanagement of funds are the major challenges to organizational efficiency in an organization.
  3. Employers motivate their employees in organizations to enhance higher performance and efficiency.
  4. Employers motivate their employees in organizations to enhance higher performance and efficiency through effective communication, giving incentives and rewards, paying salaries promptly, and giving promotions.
  5. Employees are not satisfied with the policies or ways of motivation used by the management.
  6. Recruitment, selection, and placement of wrong people into office contribute to the poor performance of the organization.

This research sought to investigate staff motivation and organization efficiency in Jos South Local Government Council. It can be concluded that staff motivation has an impact on organization efficiency. However, employees of Jos South Local Government are not satisfied with the policies or ways of motivation used by the management. Also, staff complaints are not been attended to as little attention is given to staff complaints.
