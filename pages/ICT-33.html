ICT-33: Examination of the Office Technology Skills required by OTM Students

Findings of the Research Work

The study was undertaken to examine the effect of office technology skills on students' performance.

The study revealed the following findings among others:

  1. The office technology skills required by OTM students include:
    • Ability to use various computer application software
    • Electronic mail (e-mail)
    • Machine technology skills
    • Electronic record management skills
    • Competence in the use of various telecommunication applications and devices
    • Ability to use various reprographic devices
  2. Office technology skills provide:
    • Greater exposure to vocational and workforce skills
    • Opportunities for the use of multiple technologies during training
    • Greater enthusiasm for skill acquisition amongst OTM students
    • Preparation of OTM students for the real world of work and to cope with the realities of the modern work environment
    • Encouragement of innovation and creativity in school and at the workplace
  3. The acquisition of office technology skills is challenged by factors such as:
    • Inadequate technological devices and equipment for the training of office technology managers
    • Lack of skill or capacity on the part of the trainers
    • Unwillingness to adopt new technological innovation by trainers
    • Overemphasis laid by trainers on theory instead of concentrating more on stimulating real-life experience
    • Inadequate public power supply
    • Poor and non-sustainable funding due to dwindling revenue
  4. Strategies for improved acquisition of office technology skills include:
    • Provision of adequate technological devices and equipment for the training of office technology managers
    • Technical training and retraining programs for trainers
    • Trainers should be willing to adopt new technological innovation
    • Emphasis should be placed more on practicals rather than theory during training
    • Provide alternative sources of electricity supply
    • Adopt active private sector involvement in funding OTM training
