ACC-48: The Impact of Treasury Single Account on Effective Management of Government Financial Resources in Nigeria

Findings of the Research Work

After conducting the research, the following findings were revealed:

  1. Introduction of the Treasury Single Account ensures accountability of government funds. In this research work, the researcher found that the Treasury Single Account has a positive effect on the management of funds in Plateau State Ministry of Finance.
  2. Treasury Single Account has enhanced transparency and avoids misappropriation of funds in Plateau State Ministry of Finance. Properly maintained Treasury Single Accounts can have a significant impact on fund management in the public sector.
  3. Treasury Single Account has reduced the rate of embezzlement among public office holders. The researcher discovered that the rate of embezzlement has drastically reduced in the public sector.
  4. Treasury Single Account has helped in preventing fraud in the public sector. The research found that the Treasury Single Account is a tool that prevents fraud among public office holders.
  5. Treasury Single Account has helped in controlling mismanagement of public funds. The research work revealed that a well-maintained Treasury Single Account can help eliminate the mismanagement of public funds in the public sector.
  6. An efficient public finance management system like the Treasury Single Account is required to maximize the efficient use of government resources.
