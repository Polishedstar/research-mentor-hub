MGT-16: Investment Decision and its Effect on Organizational Development

Findings of the Research Work

After careful presentation of data and analysis, the following responses were observed in Chapter four:

  1. Senior management staff makes investment decisions in the organization.
  2. The organization invests because of the interest that will accrue from such investment.
  3. From the responses, it shows that the organization is rated highly.
  4. The organization does not invest because of idle cash.
  5. It also shows from the responses that management efficiency is rated high based on decisions made.

From the research results so far, the researcher concluded that organizations should invest because of the interest that will accrue from such investment, not because of excess liquidity.
