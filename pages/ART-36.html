ART-36: Role of Mass Media in Propagating Human Rights Abuse in Nigeria (A Case Study of Silent Voices on Jay 101.9FM Jos)

Findings of the Research Work

This study primarily focuses on the topic "The role of mass media in propagating human rights abuse in Nigeria: A case study of Plateau silent voice on Jay 101.9 FM Jos." The study examines the contributions made by Plateau silent voice on Jay 101.9 FM Jos in the enforcement and promotion of human rights. It also assesses how effective Plateau silent voice on Jay 101.9 FM Jos is in the campaign against human rights abuses. The study explores the importance of promoting human rights at Jay 101.9 FM Jos and assesses the challenges that Jay 101.9 FM Jos faces regarding the promotion of human rights in Plateau State.

The study includes a review of related literature, including a conceptual review where concepts like mass media and human rights are explored. Empirical reviews were also incorporated.

For the research design, a survey research design was adopted. Both primary and secondary data were used for this study. Data collection was performed using questionnaires, and purposive sampling techniques were employed in distributing the questionnaires. The collected data were analyzed using descriptive and simple statistical methods.

The presentation and analysis of data collected from the field were completed, and responses were categorized based on personal data, which included sex, age, marital status, educational qualifications, and department. These characteristics were presented in frequency distribution tables. The responses to the questions and relevant variables, as well as counter-check questions, were analyzed and tabulated.

Findings of this study revealed that Plateau silent voice on Jay 101.9 FM Jos contributes to the enforcement and promotion of human rights. However, it was also found that Plateau silent voice on Jay 101.9 FM Jos is not very effective in the campaign against human rights abuses. The study underscores the importance of promoting human rights at Jay 101.9 FM Jos as a means to inform and educate people about their rights. Jay 101.9 FM Jos faces challenges, including limited coverage, in propagating human rights.
