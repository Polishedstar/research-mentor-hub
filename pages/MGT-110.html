MGT-110: Impact of Strategic Planning on Organizational Performance (A Case Study of Nasco Foods Nigeria Ltd, Jos)

Findings of the Research Work

This research work investigates the effect of strategic planning on organizational performance.

Chapter one introduced the research work. Strategic planning is a competitive move and a business approach that managers make use of in order to achieve the targeted levels of performance, grow the business, conduct operations, attract and please customers, and compete successfully.

Chapter two looked at the concept of strategy. Strategy is the framework which guides those choices that determine the nature and direction of the firm. The chapter reviewed the impact of strategic planning on organizational performance.

Chapter three dealt with the research methodology. The study adopted the survey research design, a population of 200 staff of Nasco Foods was studied, from which a sample size of 160 staff was used. Questionnaires were administered to the respondents, and data collected were presented in a tabular form and analyzed using simple percentages.

Chapter four dealt with the presentation and analysis of data. From the data presented and analyzed, the following were discovered:

  • Strategy is the framework which guides those choices that determine the nature and direction of the firm. Planning for business considers what is to be achieved and how it is to be achieved.
  • Strategic planning involves thinking about the future, identifying and specifying in advance what has to be done or achieved.
  • Strategic planning focus on making plans and taking actions today for the future prosperity and competitiveness of a firm.
  • Strategic planning is used to accomplish organizational goals through planning, organizing, directing, and controlling organizational resources.
  • Strategic planning is undertaken to strengthen the company’s long-term competitive position and financial performance.
  • Organizational performance is determined by the goals that were achieved in convergence of enterprise orientations.
  • Organizational performance is influenced by the environment, the objectives to be achieved, and the relevant and recognizable features.
  • Organizational performance contributes to improving the cost-value couple and not just what helps to reduce the cost or increase the value.
  • Organizational performance is a process, and not a result that occurs at a particular time.

Strategic planning of corporate bodies is an essential instrument for planning and forecasting which positions the organization to meet demands and changes that might come up in the course of discharging its services. This study reveals that Nasco Foods, Nigeria, Ltd as a corporate body has a clear strategic plan which is articulated to all of its employees at various levels and departments within the organization. It reveals that the strong agreement of factors of various dimensions of strategic planning indicate the effectiveness and efficiency of such planning adopted by employees of the organization and hence affects the organization’s performance positively.
