MGT-74: Conflict Resolution and its Effect on Organizational Productivity

Findings of the Research Work

It was discovered that poor management, lack of profound and standard strategy line basis for further training and development of employees' skills have adversely affected the efficiency and productivity of the total framework and the output of the organization as a whole.

This research has identified some key factors that affect the operational capacity of organizations. Therefore, it is necessary to establish an enabling environment within the organization where personal interests, values, religion, or tribal affiliations are set aside, and every employee adopts the values and protects the interests of the organization.
