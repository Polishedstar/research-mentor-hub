ACC-37: The Effect of Accounting Software on the Performance of Government Parastatals (A Case Study of ITF)

Findings of the Research Work

  1. Industrial Training Fund uses accounting software for its operations.
  2. Most government parastatals in Nigeria adopt accounting software for use.
  3. There is accuracy and efficiency in record keeping through the use of accounting software in the paratatal.
  4. Accounting functions like posting transactions to the ledger and double entry are simplified through the use of accounting software in the organization.
  5. Accounting software is capable of handling a large number of transactions at a time in the organization.
  6. Accounting software offers online facilities to store data.
  7. The advent of accounting software improves customer service delivery in the government parastatal.
  8. The organization has a specific computer (database) department.
  9. There are training programs organized for the organization's staff for the use of accounting software.
  10. Accounting software helps gain inherent advantages while minimizing risks involved in daily banking operations in the bank.
  11. Accounting software has an effect on the turnover and profitability of the government parastatals in Nigeria.
  12. Accounting software aids in the examination of the bank statement of financial position to ensure agreement with source documents.
  13. There are relationships between the application of accounting software and manual accounting systems in the banking industry.
  14. Computers have an impact on the transactions and record-keeping in the parastatal.
  15. There is customer satisfaction with the use of accounting software in the government parastatals.
