MGT-120: Evaluation of Corporate Planning as a Strategy for Enhancing Management Efficiency

Findings of the Research Work

Through the presentation and analysis of data collected, the following were my findings:

  • The study revealed that corporate planning helps in defining clearly organization problems and methods of achieving them in JED Plc.
  • The study also revealed that corporate planning helps in identifying the strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats of the organization.
  • The study revealed that corporate planning helps in performance evaluation and employee mental development.
  • Corporate planning helped in keeping staff in shape and improving their performance.

A thorough look at the findings of the study revealed that the evaluation of corporate planning as a strategy for enhancing management efficiency is a vital and integral tool of insurance operations, especially in modern insurance. This makes it challenging and demanding for them to meet up with their customers.

Evaluation of corporate planning helps general management to identify, define, and formulate a company's vision and mission. It also aids insurance firms in selecting performance targets and objectives that the firm wishes to achieve.

Also, it was postulated that an evaluation plan helps the management of the firm in the identification of strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats of the organization.
