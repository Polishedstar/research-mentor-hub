ART-8: Influence of Social Media on Students Writing Pattern: A Study of Eko College of Management and Technology

Findings of the Research Work

The study concluded that social media chatting has had a negative impact on students' academic writing in Nigerian higher institutions. It was found that female students tend to use social media chatting more frequently than their male counterparts.

Furthermore, the study revealed that there is no significant difference among undergraduate students in Nigeria in terms of their level of participation in chatting. However, there is a significant difference in the perception of male and female students regarding the effectiveness of social media chatting on students' English Language writing.
