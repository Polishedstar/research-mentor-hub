EDU-14: Effect of Gender Career Aspirations of Students in Secondary Schools in Jos North LGA of Plateau State

Findings of the Research Work

This study has examined the effects of gender career aspirations of students in Secondary Schools in Jos North Local Government Area of Plateau State. Below are the summary of findings:

  1. That gender career aspirations have an effect on students in secondary schools in Jos North Local Government Area of Plateau State.
  2. That there is a greater level of awareness of contemporary high-profile careers by secondary school students in Jos North Local Government Area of Plateau State.
  3. That there are differences in the career aspirations of male and female students in secondary schools in Jos North Local Government Area of Plateau State.
  4. That gender/sex role stereotyping influences secondary school girls' career aspirations to the highest extent in Jos North Local Government Area of Plateau State.
  5. That social class greatly influences students' career aspirations in secondary schools in Jos North Local Government Area of Plateau State.
  6. That subjects taught in the school have an effect on students' future career aspiration, future educational achievement, and students' future occupation, job opportunity, and endeavor.
