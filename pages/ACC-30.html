ACC-30: Conducive Environment and Workers Higher Productivity in an Organization (A Case Study of United Bank of Africa)

Findings of the Research Work

From the result of data collected and analyzed, the following summary of findings can be drawn:

  1. That the environment of the business has an impact on the company’s profit maximization.
  2. That the company makes meaningful contributions to the community where it operates.
  3. That there is sufficient patronage of the company’s product by the people.
  4. That the company has competitors, and that the presence of competitors has an adverse effect on the company’s product survival.
  5. That government policies have a great impact on the existence of the organization’s survival.
  6. That the scientific and technological environment has an effect on the organization's business.
  7. That the environment, especially the weather condition, has effects on the sales.
