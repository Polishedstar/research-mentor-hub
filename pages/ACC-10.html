ACC-10: Impact of Information Technology on the Profitability of Deposit Money Banks in Nigeria (A Case Study of First Bank of Nigeria Plc)

Findings of the Research Work

  1. Information and communication technology system has an effect on the smooth operations of the deposit money banks in Nigeria.
  2. Information and communication technology system is adopted and used by the deposit money banks in Nigeria.
  3. Posting of transactions to the ledger and double entry are simplified through the use of information and communication technology system in the banks.
  4. One of the advantages of information and communication technology system over the manual system is that it is capable of handling a large number of transactions at a time in the banks.
  5. The advent of information and communication technology system has improved customer service delivery in the banks.
  6. Information and communication technology system has a great effect on the turnover and profitability of the banks in Nigeria.
  7. Computers have an impact on the banking transactions and record-keeping in the banks.
