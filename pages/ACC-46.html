ACC-46: An Evaluation of the Effectiveness of Internal Audit in Government Establishments

Findings of the Research Work

The studies revealed the following summary of findings:

  1. The system has effective organizational planning and implementation of internal procedure.
  2. The internal audit function of the institution of policy and strategic studies is effective and efficient.

Sequel to the analysis and findings, some conclusions were derived in an attempt to ascertain the effectiveness of internal audit in government establishment.

The motive for the study was to identify the significant role of audit in the public sector, in its pursuit to achieve government-set goals and objectives. The objectives of the internal control system are fundamentally to ensure the smooth running of the organization in accordance with the policies of the management and to ensure accountability and check the rampant abuse of assets and the resources of the organization.

Lastly, the study is aimed at proffering possible solutions to the non-compliance of internal audit in government establishments.
